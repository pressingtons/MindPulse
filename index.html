How MindPulse works

Local decisions, honest limits.

MindPulse calibrates to steady room sound and looks for changing sound patterns. It can flag a possible disturbance; it does not identify emergencies, breathing, apnea, or health conditions.

1 Calibrate — build a private room-noise baseline.

2 Listen locally — no audio is streamed or uploaded.

3 Confirm — tell MindPulse when a sound was or was not your baby.
