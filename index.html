How MindPulse works

Transparent, local adaptation.

MindPulse measures activity spikes, low-to-mid level rustling, and the room’s ambient floor. It groups those signals into Deep Sleep, Restless, or Active Disturbance, then uses a small on-device Q-table to try an optional sound response.

1

Listen locally Audio samples stay in memory and are discarded immediately.

2

Choose transparently The model scores No Noise, White Noise, Pink Noise, and Soft Lullaby.

3

Remember locally Only numeric response scores are saved in local storage.

MindPulse does not detect emergencies, diagnose sleep, or provide medical guidance. Follow safe-sleep guidance and check on your baby whenever needed.
